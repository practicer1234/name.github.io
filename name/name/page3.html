ABOUT LISA SU

Lisa Su is a renowned leader in the semiconductor industry,

serving as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

and Chairperson of the Board of Advanced Micro Devices (AMD), an American

semiconductor company. She is highly regarded in the tech world for her exceptional leadership skills and deep understanding of semiconductor technology.

PRODUCT

Under Lisa Su's leadership, AMD has driven the development of many significant products and technologies, including the Ryzen processor series, Radeon graphics card series, and EPYC server processor series.
